Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Thank you for using RWKV Chat ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

To put it simply: We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

1. Information Collection

We believe that your data belongs to you. Therefore:

  • We do not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we ask for your name, email address, phone number, or location data.
  • We do not track your usage. We do not use any third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Flurry) to track your behavior within the app.
  • We do not collect device information. We do not harvest data about your device type, IP address, or advertising ID.

2. Data Storage

All data generated or processed by RWKV Chat is stored exclusively locally on your device. We do not have servers, and we do not transmit your data to any external cloud storage.

If you delete the app, any local data associated with it will be permanently removed from your device.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any information, we have no information to share, sell, or rent to third parties, advertisers, or marketing companies.

4. Permissions

If the app requests access to certain device features (such as the Camera or Photo Library), this access is used strictly for the app's functionality (e.g., to take a photo or select an image for editing). This data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your email address, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
